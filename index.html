Cola Beach Sunset Bay is the place to be to chillax!

With the back drop of terraced coconut groves Cola Beach Resort is the only beach front boutique tented resort located on a spectacular uninhabited beach and private bay in south Goa. It remains one of the only unspoiled coastal areas in Goa India affording you a holiday completely in touch with nature. Cola Beach Resort is a completely secluded and un-spoilt beach offering 12 Tents accommodations, an escape from the hustle and bustle of city life and crowded beaches elsewhere, giving you a taste of rustic luxury living.
